How to Play — The Rogers $20K Adventure

The goal is simple: save $20,000 by December 31, 2026 while racing your game piece to Space 39. Here's how everything works together.

The Savings Grid

Pick a box, cross it off. Each payday, choose a grid box you can afford. Click it to cross it off — the dollar amount gets added to your total saved. Every box you cross off also moves your game piece forward on the board (+1 space, or +2 for boxes worth $500 or more). You can uncross a box if you made a mistake.

The Game Board

Move forward by saving and completing challenges. Your game piece (the "R") moves along the winding board. You advance by crossing off savings grid boxes AND by completing weekly challenges. The goal is to reach Space 39 by the end of the year.

Weekly Challenges

Spin the wheel each week. The spinner gives you a challenge for the week — like "No DoorDash" or "Pack lunch every day." Challenges come in three difficulties:
Easy
Complete: +1 space
Miss: -1 space
Medium
Complete: +2 spaces
Miss: -1 space
Hard
Complete: +3 spaces
Miss: -2 spaces
Log your result at the end of the week. Use the "Log This Week's Challenge" section below the spinner. Hit "Nailed It" or "We Failed" — your game piece moves and streaks update automatically.

Streaks & Penalties

Winning streaks feel great. Miss streaks hurt. Complete challenges in a row and your week streak climbs. But miss 2 in a row? You lose 3 EXTRA spaces on top of the normal penalty. That's the miss streak tax — it stacks every time you fail consecutively.
Checkpoints keep you honest. Week 4: Not on the board yet? Handwritten apology letters to each other. Week 20: Below Space 15? Rewards are FROZEN. Last 8 weeks: Missed challenges cost DOUBLE penalty spaces.

Payday Planner

Figure out how much you can save each payday. Enter your checking balance, mark which bills are already paid, and the planner calculates how much is safe to save after bills, fun money, groceries, gas, and a safety buffer. It even recommends which grid box range to cross off. Use the "New Month" button to reset all bills when a new month starts. Bill amounts can be edited right in the list when they change.

Victory Conditions

Hit $20K AND reach Space 39 = DINNER OUT. No budget limit. Both of you earned it. But if you don't hit $20K by December 31? Each other's worst chore for an entire MONTH. The stakes are real.

Quick Tips

Save first, spend second. Use the Payday Planner BEFORE spending on wants. Pick your grid box at the start of each pay period, move that money to savings, then enjoy what's left guilt-free. Completing challenges moves you on the board, but so does saving — so even if you miss a challenge, a strong savings week still pushes you forward.

Saving Your Progress

Hit the Save button to keep your data. Your progress is stored in Airtable so you and Mason can access it from any device. After making changes (crossing off boxes, logging challenges, editing bills), click the green Save button in the bottom-right corner. The site loads your latest data automatically when you open it.
